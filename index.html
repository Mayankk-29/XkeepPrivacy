Privacy Policy for Xkeep: Crypto/Password Manager

Effective Date:15/03/2025

At Xkeep, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No Personal Information: Xkeep does not collect or store any personal information or passwords. All data is stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any data in the cloud or on any external servers.

2. Data We Collect

  • Encryption Data: We only store encrypted versions of your passwords and crypto wallet keys. These are encrypted locally on your device using AES encryption and PBKDF2 key derivation.
  • Quick Notes: Any notes you create are stored locally and are not encrypted unless specified by you.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Local Storage: All passwords, crypto keys, and notes are stored on your device securely. We do not transmit, store, or share this data with third parties.
  • Encryption: We use AES encryption (CBC mode) and PBKDF2 key derivation to ensure your data is secure and cannot be accessed without your master password.

4. Security

  • Encryption: Your data is encrypted with industry-standard AES encryption and PBKDF2 key derivation using a key derived from your master password. This ensures that only you have access to your data.
  • Offline Storage: All information is stored on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. We do not store any data in the cloud.

5. Data Sharing

  • No Sharing: Xkeep does not share, sell, or rent any of your personal or encrypted data to third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Analytics and Ads: Xkeep does not use third-party analytics or ads services that may collect your data. The app functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection to use its core features.

7. User Rights

  • Access to Data: You have full access to all the data stored on your device. You can view, edit, or delete your stored data at any time within the app.
